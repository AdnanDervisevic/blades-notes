# Silkshore

The city's "red lamp district" — district guide, p. 274.

Criss-crossed by dozens of narrow canals, best navigated by gondola. Brothels, vice dens, food stalls, and exotic shops perch at the waterside, "ready to satisfy the appetites of their clientele, no questions asked." A place of public indulgence and private indelicacy (p. 274).

## Relevance to The Creed

**Our lair is here** — above a brothel (specific one not yet chosen). Hunting grounds are in [[Brightstone]].

Note: the book's **Red Lamp** is a specific brothel here — "the oldest and most respected institution of its sort in the city," operated by **Madame Tesslyn** (p. 275). Candidate for our lair, or a neighbor/rival to whichever brothel we pick.

## Mechanical note (p. 275)

Overindulge your vice in Silkshore and you get a taste for it: +1d to your next indulge-vice roll here.

## Notable NPCs (p. 275)

- **Madame Tesslyn** — operates the Red Lamp brothel
- **Helene** — elegant, mysterious proprietor of the Silver Stag Casino; "would have been a queen of Severos" in the old days
- **Levyra** — lets ghosts in bottles possess her for final words with the bereaved

## Landmarks include

The Spark Grounds — open field beneath a lightning tower; acrobats, circus performers, and the notorious "spark flyers" (p. 274).

# Brightstone

[[The Creed]]'s hunting grounds. District guide: p. 258.

Home to many of the wealthiest and most influential citizens of [[Doskvol]]. Broad paved streets under bright electric lights; sparkling canals with perfumed water; pale marble mansions; cultivated parks fed by radiant energy; lavish restaurants, jewelers, tailors, luxury shops. Street-side vendors forbidden — serene and spacious, punctuated by carriages and Bluecoat patrols.

## Traits (p. 258)

Wealth: very high. Security & Safety: very high. Criminal influence: low. Occult influence: low.

## Mechanical notes (p. 259)

- Most engagement rolls suffer **-1d** due to heavy Bluecoat patrols
- Operations against the nobility here count as **hostile turf** for heat

## Landmarks (p. 258)

1. **Unity Park** — grand park with monument to Imperial victory in the Unity War (or "the War for Skovlan Independence, depending on who you talk to")
2. **Silver Market** — curated open-air luxury emporium overlooking the North Hook channel; Iruvian silks, Dagger Isles spices, rare alchemical distillations including some illegal spirit essences the Watch ignores
3. **The Sanctorium** — chief cathedral of the Church of the Ecstasy of the Flesh; catacombs hold the cremated ash of the famous and affluent
4. **Bowmore Bridge** — said to be the largest bridge in the Imperium; luxury apartments and shops along its length from Brightstone to Whitecrown

## Streets

Silvermark Avenue, Goldcrest Avenue, Ruby Street, Sapphire Street — broad, clean, well-lit (p. 258).

## Why it matters to us

Rich targets, elite clients, heavy law. A gentleman's manners ([[Laudius Kinclaith]]) are a working tool here.

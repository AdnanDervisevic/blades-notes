# The Brothel (Lair)

[[The Creed]]'s base of operations: a brothel in [[Silkshore]], among the canals of the Ease. The crew occupies the **fancy, off-limits top floor** of the building — not a dinky hovel, but a space they can't really afford to keep up. ("We have standards, but not the funds to meet those standards in a proper way.")

## Ties

- [[Ardeshir Ahreman]] ("Loom") helped keep the struggling brothel's business afloat, earning his name; [[Irimina]] has a connection to the place and introduced him to it.
- When [[Layla Nemat]] needed a base, Irimina directed her here to Loom.
- [[Holtz Welker]] and the Kinclaith cousins laid low here and met Layla and Ardeshir.
- The crew's agreement with the house: if the brothel has a problematic client, the crew helps solve it.

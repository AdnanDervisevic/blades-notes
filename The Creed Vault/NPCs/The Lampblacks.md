# The Lampblacks

A Tier II faction; the former lamp-lighter guild, turned to crime when their services were replaced by electric lights (p. 287). Led by [[Bazso Baz]]. Doskvol-native criminals, at war with the Iruvian-backed [[The Red Sashes|Red Sashes]].

## Why they matter to The Creed

The faction [[The Creed]] holds **-2** standing with. The crew's grudge is personal as well as factional: the rigged fight run by [[Laudius Kinclaith]], [[Holtz Welker]], and financed by [[Irimina]] was aimed squarely at hurting the Lampblacks — making them lose money and face — and it humiliated Bazso when Laudius' scam came to light. They remember it.

## Book facts (p. 287)

- **Goal:** Destroy the Red Sashes; become ward boss of Crow's Foot
- **Leader:** [[Bazso Baz]] — charming, open, ruthless; whiskey connoisseur
- **Lieutenants:** Pickett (shrewd, conniving), Henner (loyal, reckless thug)
- **Turf:** HQ in a coal warehouse office; brothels and cheap drug dens across Crow's Foot
- **Allies:** The Fog Hounds, Gondoliers, Ironhook Prison
- **Enemies:** The Red Sashes, Bluecoats, Cabbies
- **Quirk:** Bazso secretly belongs to a forgotten-gods cult, "The Empty Vessel" (see [[Holtz Welker]] — our table has built its own doctrine for this cult)
- **Reputation:** folk-hero "lovable rogues" among the working class

The Lampblacks–Red Sashes war is canon (p. 204, 287).

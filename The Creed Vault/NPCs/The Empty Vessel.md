# The Empty Vessel

A forgotten-gods cult. The faith of [[Holtz Welker]] ("Shepherd") and his follower [[Marlane]]; also Holtz's **Faith** vice.

## Doctrine (our table's build)

All living beings are hollow husks. Only when a husk is broken enough can it be filled with an actual soul. In moments of severe pain or injury — or in the instant before a gruesome death — the husk cracks open and the person can finally feel the truth.

This creates the central dilemma: to kill yourself ends the revelation, and then no one will know the truth. So the calling is to **preserve and spread the truth** — to break husks open without simply destroying them. Holtz styles himself "Shepherd" to this end.

## Book note

In the core book, "The Empty Vessel" is the forgotten-gods cult secretly followed by [[Bazso Baz]] of the [[The Lampblacks|Lampblacks]] (p. 287). Our table has written the doctrine above for it. Whether Holtz and Bazso truly share the same cult — an awkward link, given their fight-rigging history — is an open thread worth raising with the GM.

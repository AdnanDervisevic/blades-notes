# Bazso Baz

**Enemy of [[Laudius Kinclaith]]** — the "gang leader" entry on the Slide's friends/rivals list (p. 76).

## Our history

Bazso organized the fighting pit where Laudius fought. When Laudius' fight-throwing scam was exposed, the pit got shut down — and it showed everyone that Bazso had no idea what was going on right under his nose. Laudius cost him money *and* face. Bazso doesn't forget either.

## Book facts

Leader of **the Lampblacks** (Tier II faction, p. 287) — the former lamp-lighter guild, turned to crime when electric lights replaced their services.

- Personality: "charming, open, and ruthless" — and "very sharp" (pp. 220, 287). A whiskey connoisseur.
- Turf: HQ in the office of a coal warehouse; operates a handful of brothels and cheap drug dens across Crow's Foot (p. 287)
- Lieutenants: Pickett (second — shrewd, conniving, suspicious), Henner (thug — loyal, reckless)
- Quirk: secret member of a forgotten-gods cult, "The Empty Vessel," whose needs he sometimes puts ahead of his gang (p. 287)
- Allies: The Fog Hounds, Gondoliers, **Ironhook Prison** (p. 287)
- Enemies: The Red Sashes, The Bluecoats, Cabbies (p. 287)
- Situation: at war with the Red Sashes over turf and vengeance; recruiting every free blade in the district, "doesn't take no for an answer" (p. 287). Working class sees the Lampblacks as folk-hero "lovable rogues."

## Hooks for The Creed

- Book's Assassins hook (p. 220): Bazso may want the crew to kill Mylera's contact at the Iruvian Consulate, who funnels Iruvian government money to the Red Sashes. Awkward if he learns Silk is in the crew — or leverage.
- His allies include [[Ironhook Prison]] — Laudius did time there. Did Bazso's reach follow him inside? Was the prison fight ring connected to Bazso's network? Is [[Harker]] known to him?
- He runs brothels (Crow's Foot, not Silkshore) — he knows that business. Could he find our lair through it?

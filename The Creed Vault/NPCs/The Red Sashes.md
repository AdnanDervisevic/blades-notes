# The Red Sashes

A Tier II faction; originally a school of ancient Iruvian sword arts, since expanded into criminal endeavors (p. 287). An Iruvian proxy in Doskvol's underworld and the sworn enemy of [[The Lampblacks]].

## Why they matter to The Creed

The Red Sashes are the underworld arm of Iruvian interests, allied to the **Iruvian Consulate** — the faction [[The Creed]] holds **+2** standing with. Several Red Sashes are sons and daughters of Iruvian nobility and diplomats, trained at the school, whose powerful families "will commit significant resources to punishing anyone who harms their children" (p. 287). Their war with the Lampblacks is the conflict the whole crew is, separately, already tangled in.

## Book facts (p. 287)

- **Goal:** Destroy the Lampblacks; become ward boss of Crow's Foot
- **Leader:** Mylera Klev — shrewd, ruthless, educated, an art collector
- **Turf:** HQ in their sword-fighting school/temple; high-end drug dens across Crow's Foot and the Docks
- **Assets:** a small contingent of master sword-fighters; a master alchemist with potent potions and essences
- **Allies:** Iruvian Consulate, the Path of Echoes, Dockers, Cabbies, Inspectors
- **Enemies:** The Lampblacks, Bluecoats, Gondoliers
- **Situation:** at war with the Lampblacks over turf and vengeance; Mylera is pressing every free blade into service. Very well-connected, with former students placed in the Consulate, the Path of Echoes, and among the Inspectors.

The Lampblacks–Red Sashes war is canon; the open-war scenario is detailed in "Running the Game" (p. 204).

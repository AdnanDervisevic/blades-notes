# Marlane

A former military cadet who became a follower of [[Holtz Welker]] and a convert to [[The Empty Vessel]]. Now a pugilist fighting in the docks rings under Holtz's teachings, seeking the truth of the faith through pain given and received.

Marlane was the fighter in the rigged match: approached by [[Irimina]] and [[Laudius Kinclaith]] alongside Holtz, Marlane took repeated beatings to build long odds, then knocked out the favourite in a high-odds reversal — the upset that hurt the [[The Lampblacks|Lampblacks]] and gave the crew its -2 standing.

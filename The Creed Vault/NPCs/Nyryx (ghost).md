# Nyryx (possessor ghost)

A possessor ghost; rival and personal villain of [[Victoria Kinclaith]].

> Note: distinct from the Nyryx on the Slide playbook's contact list (a prostitute, p. 76). Same name, different being.

A spirit that slipped its tether to the Ghost Field and learned to wear living flesh like a borrowed coat. It was once bound in service to the Kinclaith household — a familiar summoned, leashed, and made to answer. Nyryx was present the night [[Victoria Kinclaith|Victoria]]'s husband, the Duke, died; whether it was the instrument of that death or merely the thing that walked out of the room afterward, she has never been certain. Its binding broke in the same hour the Duke's heart stopped.

Now Nyryx moves freely through Doskvol and has not forgotten the hand that once held its chain. It haunts Victoria with the faces of people she trusts, speaking in the cadence of her dead husband, always a step ahead of the veil she believes she controls. It wants her to lift that boundary fully — because a Whisper who *becomes* the veil is a door, and Nyryx fully intends to walk back through.

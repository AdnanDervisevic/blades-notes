# Quellyn

A witch, healer, and seer; mentor to [[Victoria Kinclaith]].

[[Victoria Kinclaith|Victoria]] met Quellyn on the cold edge of Tycheros, beyond the Void Sea, arriving grieving and adrift. The witch saw in her an aptitude she had not yet recognised — a thinness to her, a widow's natural proximity to the other side — and took her in, teaching her to read the tarot as a discipline rather than parlour theatre and opening her eyes to the occult machinery beneath the human world: the Ghost Field, the hungry dead, and the price of every answer drawn from beyond.

It was Quellyn who eventually sent Victoria back out to "venture the world." Whether that was generosity, a completed apprenticeship, or a debt being set up for later collection is a question Victoria has chosen not to examine too closely; gifts from seers rarely come without an entry in some ledger.

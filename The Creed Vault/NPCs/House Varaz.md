# House Varaz

An Iruvian noble house, now destroyed. Former masters of [[Ardeshir Ahreman]], who served as its house steward.

The house was attacked without warning — knives in the dark and soldiers in the courtyard — almost certainly by a rival house. [[Ardeshir Ahreman|Ardeshir]] escaped with his lover [[Jennah]] and fled to Doskvol. He has found no evidence that any full member of the house survived, and seeks vengeance on his masters' behalf.

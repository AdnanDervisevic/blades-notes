# Victoria Kinclaith

Akorosi noblewoman, widow, and occultist, known as **"The Veil."** Cousin to [[Laudius Kinclaith]]. A Whisper who reads the tarot and consorts with the dead. Member of [[The Creed]].

## Appearance

A traditionally beautiful woman with distinct features: a sharp jaw and thin eyebrows paired with round brown eyes that would look innocent were it not for the unnatural shade of her black hair, tied in a tight bun — at close glance it appears treated, as if with pomade or silver nitrate, to darken its colour.

She dresses in long gowns of darker colours and looks unremarkable but for her hands, which are decorated with several exotic golden rings and an intricate dark-brown henna pattern twisting along her left wrist and down her ring and pinky fingers. A brown leather holster of square pattern is fixed to her dress, holding a golden tarot deck. One would not realise she is an Akorosi woman until she speaks; her look is that of a refined entertainer from a travelling circus.

## History

Born into a noble household, Victoria was married to a Duke and widowed young. Left with a respectable estate and wealth but scorned by her peers for her widowhood, she chose to travel and explore the world, journeying as far as Tycheros, beyond the northern Void Sea. Along the way she shifted her appearance away from that of the refined noble lady and developed a fixation on tarot and the macabre, as though seeking answers beyond the human realm. It was on the cold edge of Tycheros that she met the witch [[Quellyn]], who took her in, taught her to read the tarot as a discipline, and opened her eyes to the occult machinery beneath the world.

When her cousin Laudius was incarcerated at [[Ironhook Prison]], Victoria — finding his situation intriguing and seeking to flee her boredom — returned to settle once more in Doskvol.

## The name "The Veil"

As a widow, she is said to lift the veil between the two worlds. The title is not merely a metaphor but a living boundary: those who speak it mean the thin, breathing membrane between worlds where the dead hesitate and the future leaks through. In whispered accounts she does not only lift the veil — she becomes it, and eyes that meet hers briefly forget which side of existence they are on.

## Vice

**Weird** — consorting with rogue spirits. Her purveyor is **[[Salia]]**, a spirit of the Reconciled which moves from body to body at its whim (a canon purveyor, p. 243).

## Relationships

- **[[Quellyn]]** — witch, healer, and seer; her mentor, met beyond the Void Sea.
- **[[Nyryx (ghost)|Nyryx]]** — a possessor ghost once bound to the Kinclaith household; her rival and personal villain.

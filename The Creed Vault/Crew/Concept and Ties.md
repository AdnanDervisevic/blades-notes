# The Creed — Concept & Ties

The shared framing the group agreed on in pre-game discussion. We start *in medias res*, so exact relationships can still be explored in play.

## Elevator pitch

> Five people who can pass in high society but command none of its power, gathered in a fixer's brothel and pointed by an Iruvian handler at a war they each, separately, already had a stake in.

## Why Tier 0 despite all the high-society connections

The crew can move in elite circles but has no money or power to wield. Each member's access is blocked or spent:

- **[[Layla Nemat]]** — a *deniable asset* of her Iruvian house; she cannot use her high-society connections publicly or for funding.
- **[[Ardeshir Ahreman]]** ("Loom") — has no real connections left; his house ([[House Varaz]]) is dead.
- **[[Holtz Welker]]** — a disgraced officer sunk into the lower levels of society, where he met [[Laudius Kinclaith]].
- **[[Laudius Kinclaith]]** & **[[Victoria Kinclaith]]** — the Kinclaith cousins, with standing but no leverage.

The only way "up" for now is through [[Irimina]] as a handler. She can tie the crew to both high and low society, because Iruvia has interests in both.

## The relationship web

- **Laudius ↔ Victoria** — cousins.
- **Laudius ↔ Holtz** — connected through the fixed fight / rigging scandal at the docks rings.
- **Layla ↔ Ardeshir** — connected through Iruvian nobility; [[Irimina]] may have been the matchmaker who put them in touch. Ardeshir is keen to keep Iruvian political ties active to learn what happened to his house.
- **[[Irimina]] is the glue.** She had several operations running and now wants to merge two of them into a new gig:
  - She has a connection to the brothel and introduced **Loom** to it; he helped keep it afloat.
  - When **Layla** needed a base of operations, Irimina directed her to Loom and the brothel.
  - **Holtz and the Kinclaith cousins** laid low at the brothel (by accident, or knowing the owner from before) and met Layla and Ardeshir there; the group realized they had complementary skillsets. Holtz and the cousins, more desperate to make a buck, were brought into the plans.
  - Irimina already knew Holtz and Laudius because she **financed the rigged fight** that targeted the [[The Lampblacks|Lampblacks]] — making them lose money and resources. That rig was, in effect, her last operation before assembling this crew.

## The conflict they're all tied to

- **[[The Red Sashes]]** — an Iruvian proxy gang, tied to the Iruvian Consulate (the crew's **+2** faction).
- **[[The Lampblacks]]** — Doskvol-native criminals (the crew's **-2** faction), holding a grudge against Laudius and Holtz.
- The Red Sashes and Lampblacks are canonically at war (p. 204). The crew is connected to both sides and to the higher Iruvian-Consulate-level political struggle above them.

# Slide Playbook

"A subtle manipulator and spy" (p. 76). Played by [[Laudius Kinclaith]].

## XP trigger

Earn xp when you address a challenge with **deception or influence**. "Talk your way into trouble, then talk your way out again. Pretend to be someone you're not. Bluff, lie, and manipulate to get your way." (p. 76)

## Starting actions

Sway 2, Consort 1.

## Friends / rivals (p. 76)

- Bryl, a drug dealer
- Bazso Baz, a gang leader
- Klyra, a tavern owner
- Nyryx, a prostitute
- **[[Harker]], a jail bird** — our friend

## Template builds (p. 76)

The Grifter (Consort +1, Finesse +2, Study +1, ability: A Little Something on the Side), The Siren, The Prestige, The Spy — templates are optional guidance only.

## To look up when choosing special ability

Slide abilities list (p. 77): Rook's Gambit, Cloak & Dagger, Ghost Voice, Like Looking into a Mirror, A Little Something on the Side, Mesmerism, Subterfuge, Trust in Me.
